# Quizzes

Worth 10% of Final Grade

At the start of nearly every class, your instructor will EITHER be asking a few questions about the videos and readings assigned the previous week OR calling on students to ask a question about the the videos and readings assigned the previous week.

Students need to raise their hand to answer. The instructor will ask the first person with their hand raised for their answer.

Each correct answer is worth 5 of the 10 marks. Over the course of the semester you need to answer 2 questions to get your full 10%.

# Hybrid Asynchronous Work

Worth 20% of Final Grade

The Hybrid Exercises are deliverables that you will complete entirely outside of class hours. These exercises all reference the course website modules for the first four weeks. You will need to budget your time outside class to complete this work. The Hybrid portion of this course is the asynchronous part of the course and represents roughly 2 hours per week of work.
